withe the wide spread adoption of view point units in browsers, we can finally achieve what i call fluid tipography. By this, i mean type that inherently fits in its surroundings, not by reflowing, but by resizing,keeping size relationships, line heights, and copy-fitting intact. this shift means less caveats in our design, and in our markup. less work, for better output, resulting in more project time to worry about things like performance budgets.
